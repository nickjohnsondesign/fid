Tapenade (Olive Spread)

This is a really simple dish to prepare and it's always a big hit at parties. My father recommends:

Make this the night before so that the flavors have time to blend. Just bring it up to room temperature before you serve it.

Ingredients

  • 8oz. jar sundried tomatoes
  • 2 large garlic cloves
  • 2/3 c. kalamaata olives
  • 1 t. capers

Instructions

  1. Combine tomatoes and garlic in a food processor. Blend until as smooth as possible
  2. Add capers and olives. Pule the motor a few times until they are incorporated, but still retain some texture
  3. Serve on thin toast rounds with goat cheese and fresh basil garnish (optional).
